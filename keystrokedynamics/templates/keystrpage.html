About the Project and How To Start:

Keystroke Dymanics is a web application represent algorithm of authentication users via keystroke. This web applicarion has two important parts. First part creates your unique keystroke profile and store it on server (we named it "Create Profile"). When you finished first part you'll be able to compare your live typing with submitted keystroke. Basically, second part is authentication. In this part application will try to recognize you.

What is Keystroke Dynamics?

Keystroke dynamics, keystroke biometrics or typing dynamics, is the detailed timing information that describes exactly when each key was pressed and when it was released as a person is typing at a computer keyboard. (Wikipedia)
The behavioral biometric of Keystroke Dynamics uses the manner and rhythm in which an individual types characters on a keyboard or keypad. The keystroke rhythms of a user are measured to develop a unique b iometric template of the user's typing pattern for future authentication...
